Teen Store window concept

For this project, we were tasked with designing several store window concepts, each reflecting a unique theme and target audience. The first concept I created centers around the theme of "Teenagers," aiming to capture the energy, style, and interests of this dynamic demographic. I wanted the window display to resonate with the vibrancy and individuality often associated with teenage culture. To achieve this, I incorporated bold colors, modern design elements, and products that appeal to the diverse tastes of teenagers, from fashion and accessories to gadgets and lifestyle items. The layout and styling of the window were intended to evoke a sense of fun and excitement, while also staying current with the latest trends that shape teen culture. By using eye-catching visuals and playful displays, I aimed to create an inviting atmosphere that would draw the attention of passersby and appeal directly to a younger audience, while also being versatile enough to adapt to different seasonal or promotional themes.

Extra information

Used tools Blender, Sketchup, Gimp.
Main task Create a few different types of store windows with different themes.
Starting date Autumn of 2022.
Finished date End of 2023.
